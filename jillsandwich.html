Jill Sandwich

Making a Jill Sandwich is one of the simplest sandwiches a person can make.

Quite popular among Resident Evil fans, this recipe is both cheap and easy for beginners to cooking.

Ingredients

  • Jill Valentine
  • Room with spiky ceiling contraption
  • T-virus
  • Zombie Dogs
  • Shotgun

Directions

  1. Let a virus loose in a remote mansion in the mountains. This will prompt your Jill to investigate your mansion.
  2. Let a pack of wild dogs loose in your front yard, Doing so will force the Jill to seek shelter in your mansion.
  3. Place a shotgun on a wall mount. When removed (which is bound to happen), this will cause the ceiling to cave in on your Jill, leaving you with a nice flat Jill Sandwich
  4. Enjoy!
