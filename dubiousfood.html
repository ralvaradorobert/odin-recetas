Dubious Food

Description

You can create this wonderful dish with pretty much anything, making it the perfect dish to cook when you're on the go. Perfect for those who live a busy lifestyle!

Wanna know how to make this yourself? Read on below!

Ingredients

  • Apple
  • Carrots
  • Raw Meat
  • Monster Horn
  • Stamella Mushroom
  • Armored Carp
  • Ironshroom
  • Fairy

Instructions

  1. Turn on your slow cooker and set it to high.
  2. Throw in the ingredients in any order you see fit. There is no need to chop them.
  3. After 2 seconds your dish should be ready to serve!
