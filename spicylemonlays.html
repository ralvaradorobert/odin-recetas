Spicy Lemon Lays

Description

I've been eating spicy lemon lays since I was a youngling. My mom would always prepare them and share them with me. Very hard to resist!

Preparing them is simple! Simply put whatever amount of Lays potato chips you want to eat into a bowl. Put as much Tapatio as you'd like, but beware because it can be spicy. And lastly, cut open a lemon and use a lemon squeezer to squeeze the juice over the lemon lays. Try not to use more than one, so it does not mask the spiciness of the Tapatio.

And Voila! You're done!

Ingredients

  • Potato Chips (Lays work best)
  • Lemon or Lime
  • Tapatio sauce (can also use Valentina or Cholula)

Instructions

  1. Put potato chips into a bowl
  2. Cut a lemon in half and use a lemon squeezer to press lemon juice onto chips.
  3. Throw some hot sauce of your choice (Tapatio preferred) on your chips.
  4. Grab a fork and dig in!
